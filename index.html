Why create WOO-VIES?

Despite the abundance of streaming services available, most people have a hard time figuring out what they want to watch. Some find themselves wasting more time endlessly scrolling for the next movie rather than spend time watching it. WOO-VIES seeks to make this process easy and effortless, by providing a recommendation system that is tailored to the userâ€™s preferences. Although most streaming services do have built-in recommendation systems, they draw from a limited collection of content and thus do not always provide relevant recommendations. We believe users need a separate application that can provide a wider collection of films and TV shows for users to choose from.
